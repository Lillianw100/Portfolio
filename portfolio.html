About Me:

Hello! My name is Lillian Watkins and I am from Charlottesville Virginia. I am a Junior in the School of Media Arts and Design at James Madison University. I have a concentration in Interactive Design, and a minor in Creative Writing. Throughout my time here at JMU, I have fallen in love with the concept of using the internet as a means of communication. I have been allowed the opportunity to tap into my creative side and create content and web design that reflects myself as an individual. Although most of my work that is showcased here has been completed for school, I enjoy finding time outside of that letting my creativity take control, especially with writing.

Graphics

Writings
